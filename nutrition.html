Healthy Breakfast

Start your day with nutritious, energy-boosting meals

Overnight Oats

Easy
5 min 1 serving
Ingredients:
  • Rolled oats
  • Greek yogurt
  • Chia seeds
  • Berries
  • Honey

Instructions:
Mix oats, yogurt, and chia seeds. Add berries and honey. Refrigerate overnight.

Avocado Toast

Easy
10 min 1 serving
Ingredients:
  • Whole grain bread
  • Avocado
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Red pepper flakes

Instructions:
Toast bread. Mash avocado with lemon and salt. Spread on toast, sprinkle with red pepper flakes.

Protein Smoothie

Easy
5 min 1 serving
Ingredients:
  • Banana
  • Spinach
  • Protein powder
  • Almond milk
  • Peanut butter

Instructions:
Blend all ingredients until smooth. Add ice for desired consistency.

Nutritious Lunch

Balanced midday meals to keep you energized

Quinoa Buddha Bowl

Medium
20 min 2 servings
Ingredients:
  • Quinoa
  • Chickpeas
  • Kale
  • Sweet potato
  • Tahini dressing

Instructions:
Cook quinoa and roast sweet potato. Massage kale. Combine with chickpeas and tahini dressing.

Grilled Chicken Salad

Medium
25 min 2 servings
Ingredients:
  • Chicken breast
  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil vinaigrette

Instructions:
Grill seasoned chicken. Combine greens, tomatoes, cucumber. Top with sliced chicken and dressing.

Lentil Soup

Medium
30 min 4 servings
Ingredients:
  • Red lentils
  • Vegetables
  • Vegetable broth
  • Herbs
  • Spices

Instructions:
Sauté vegetables, add lentils and broth. Simmer until lentils are tender. Season to taste.

Essential Nutrition Tips

💧
Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Add lemon or cucumber for flavor.

🍽️
Portion Control

Use smaller plates and listen to hunger cues. Eat slowly and mindfully.

📦
Meal Prep

Prepare meals in advance to avoid unhealthy choices during busy days.

⚖️
Balanced Macros

Include protein, healthy fats, and complex carbs in each meal.

Healthy Eating Guidelines

🍏 Foods to Include

  • Lean proteins (chicken, fish, legumes)
  • Whole grains (quinoa, brown rice, oats)
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Healthy fats (avocado, nuts, olive oil)
  • Plenty of water

🚫 Foods to Limit

  • Processed and packaged foods
  • Sugary drinks and snacks
  • Refined grains and white bread
  • Excessive saturated fats
  • High sodium foods
